International Shipping Policy

International shipping times vary by destination, carrier network, customs processing, local holidays, weather, and import inspection. Delivery estimates shown at checkout are estimates and may change after the shipment enters the destination country.

Customers are responsible for duties, taxes, brokerage fees, and import fees unless checkout clearly states that these charges are prepaid. Support should not quote exact customs charges because they are determined by the destination country.

Tracking may show limited updates while a package is between countries or waiting for customs clearance. If tracking has no update for fewer than 14 days, support should explain that international tracking gaps can happen. If tracking has no update for more than 14 days, escalate for carrier investigation.

International address changes after shipment are rarely possible. If the order has not shipped, support may request an address correction. If the order has shipped, the customer may need to contact the carrier or local postal service.

Refused international packages may be returned to sender, abandoned, or destroyed depending on the carrier and customs rules. Refunds for refused packages exclude non-refundable shipping costs, duties, taxes, and return fees unless the refusal was caused by store error.

International returns and exchanges require human review because return labels, customs declarations, tax recovery, and replacement shipping costs vary by destination.
