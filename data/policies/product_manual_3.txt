Product Manual 3: Fitness Water Bottle

The Fitness Water Bottle includes a leak-resistant lid, carry loop, measurement marks, and removable silicone seal. Wash all parts before first use.

The lid is top-rack dishwasher safe, but the bottle body should be hand washed to preserve exterior finish and measurement markings. Use mild soap and warm water. Do not use bleach, abrasive scrubbers, or harsh chemicals.

Do not microwave, freeze while full, boil, or use with carbonated beverages unless the product page specifically allows it. Pressure buildup can cause leaks or lid release.

If the bottle leaks, remove and reseat the silicone seal, check for cracks, and confirm the lid is fully tightened. Replacement lids or seals may be requested if the seal fails under warranty.

Warranty covers manufacturing defects such as cracked lid on arrival, defective seal, or faulty carry loop. Damage from drops, impacts, misuse, odor from poor cleaning, or normal scratches is not covered.
