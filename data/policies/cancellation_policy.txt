Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled before fulfillment begins. Support should check the fulfillment status before confirming cancellation. If the order is still unfulfilled, cancellation can usually be processed and the customer should receive confirmation by email.

After fulfillment starts, cancellation is not guaranteed. If the warehouse has picked, packed, or handed the shipment to the carrier, support should explain that the order may need to be delivered first and then returned under the return policy.

Cancellation requests for digital products, gift cards, personalized items, or made-to-order products require human review because these items may be created or delivered immediately after purchase.

If a customer entered an incorrect address, support should try an address change before cancellation when the order is not fulfilled. If the order has shipped, support should direct the customer to the carrier or escalate the case.

Subscription cancellations apply only to future billing cycles. A customer who wants to cancel an order already generated by a subscription must follow the normal order cancellation window.

Urgent cancellation messages should be prioritized when the order is less than 2 hours old, but support must not guarantee cancellation until fulfillment status is verified.
