Product Manual 2: Wireless Desk Lamp

The Wireless Desk Lamp includes adjustable brightness, touch power control, rechargeable battery, USB-C charging, and warm-neutral light modes. Charge fully before first use.

Use only a 5V USB-C adapter and the included cable or a certified equivalent. High-voltage fast chargers may cause overheating or battery damage. Do not use the lamp while it is wet or near water.

Tap the power button to turn the lamp on or off. Long press the power button to adjust brightness. Some models cycle light temperature by double tapping the control area.

If battery life is shorter than expected, fully charge the lamp, unplug it, and test on medium brightness. Very high brightness drains the battery faster. If the lamp does not charge, test another cable and adapter.

Stop use and contact support if the battery overheats, swells, smells unusual, smokes, sparks, or leaks. Safety-related cases must be escalated immediately.
