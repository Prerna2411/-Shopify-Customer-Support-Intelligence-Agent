Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund when an order is cancelled before fulfillment, when an eligible delivered item is returned in acceptable condition, or when a shipment is delayed by more than 7 calendar days beyond the promised delivery date. A refund request must include the order number, customer email, and a short explanation of the issue.

For delayed shipments, support should first check the carrier tracking status, the promised delivery date, and whether the package is still moving through the carrier network. If the order is delayed by 7 days or less, the customer is not automatically eligible for a refund. In that case, provide the latest delivery estimate, apologize for the inconvenience, and keep the ticket open for follow-up.

For delivered orders, refunds are approved only after the returned item is received and inspected. Items must be unused, complete, and in original packaging unless the refund is related to damage, defect, or store error. If an item is missing accessories or shows signs of use, support may offer a partial refund or escalate the case for human review.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days after approval. Bank processing times may vary. Store credit may be offered when the customer requests it or when the original payment method is no longer available.

Shipping fees are refundable only when the delay, incorrect shipment, or delivery failure was caused by the store or carrier. Shipping fees are not refunded when the customer entered an incorrect address, missed carrier delivery attempts, or refused delivery without prior approval.

High-value orders, suspected fraud, duplicate refund requests, chargeback threats, or abusive customer messages must be escalated to a human support lead before any refund promise is made.
