Exchange Policy

Exchanges are available within 30 days of delivery for eligible unused items. The exchange window begins on the carrier delivery date. Support should confirm the product, original variant, requested replacement variant, and reason for exchange.

Size, color, and style exchanges depend on current inventory. If the requested replacement is in stock, support may reserve or create the replacement order according to store procedures. If inventory is low, support should avoid promising availability until the exchange is approved.

The original item must be returned unused, clean, and in original packaging. Customers should include all accessories, labels, manuals, and protective inserts. Items that are damaged, used, or missing components may not qualify for exchange.

If the requested replacement is unavailable, the customer may choose a return, store credit, or waitlist notification. Price differences may apply when the requested item has a different price than the original item.

Exchanges for damaged, defective, or incorrect items should be treated as store-responsibility cases. Support should request photos and may provide a prepaid return label.

International exchanges require human review because return shipping, customs duties, and replacement shipping costs vary by destination.
