Membership Policy

Members receive early access to selected promotions, saved preferences, order history features, loyalty rewards, birthday offers, and limited product launches where available. Benefits may vary by country, account status, and campaign.

Membership benefits cannot be exchanged for cash. Discounts, points, or rewards cannot be transferred between accounts unless support approves an account merge for verified duplicate accounts.

Reward points may take up to 48 hours to appear after purchase. Points may be reversed if an order is cancelled, refunded, returned, marked fraudulent, or adjusted after purchase.

Members are responsible for keeping their account email current. If a customer checks out as a guest, points may not be applied automatically. Support may help attach an eligible order to a member account after verification.

Promotional rewards may have expiration dates, product exclusions, minimum order values, and stacking limitations. Support should check the campaign terms before promising reward reinstatement.

Suspected abuse, repeated account creation, referral fraud, or reward manipulation must be escalated before credits are issued.
