FAQ: What should I do if my item arrived damaged?

Send photos of the damaged item, exterior packaging, interior packaging, and shipping label within 7 days of delivery. Include the order number and a short description of the damage.

Support should determine whether the damage appears to be carrier damage, manufacturing defect, missing component, or wrong item. For safety issues such as overheating electronics, broken glass, sharp parts, smoke, or exposed wires, advise the customer to stop using the product and escalate immediately.

Possible resolutions include replacement, replacement part, refund, store credit, or warranty review. Do not require the customer to return a dangerous item without human review.
