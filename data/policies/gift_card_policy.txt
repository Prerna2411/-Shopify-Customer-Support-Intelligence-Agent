Gift Card Policy

Gift cards are delivered by email and can be used at checkout for eligible products. Delivery may take several minutes after purchase. Customers should check spam, promotions, and alternate email inboxes before reporting a missing gift card.

Gift cards do not expire unless required by local law. They cannot be replaced with cash unless required by law and cannot be used to buy another gift card.

Lost gift cards can be reissued only after the original purchaser verifies the purchase. Verification may include order number, purchaser email, billing name, and partial payment details that do not expose sensitive card information.

If a gift card code was sent to the wrong email address, support may resend it to the correct address only after confirming that the balance has not been used. If any balance has been redeemed, the case must be escalated.

Gift card balances can be combined with another payment method when the gift card balance is less than the order total. Refunds for purchases made with a gift card are normally returned to the gift card first.

Suspected stolen, resold, or fraudulently obtained gift cards must be escalated before any balance information is disclosed.
