Warranty Policy

Products include a 1 year limited warranty for manufacturing defects beginning on the delivery date. Warranty coverage applies to defects in materials, workmanship, electronics, stitching, seams, closures, charging ports, and other covered components described in the product manual.

Warranty claims require proof of purchase, the order number, photos or video of the defect, and a description of how the issue occurred. Support should ask when the issue began and whether the product was used according to the product manual.

Damage from misuse, accidents, improper cleaning, unauthorized repairs, modification, normal wear, cosmetic scratches, water exposure beyond stated product limits, or lost parts is not covered.

Approved warranty resolutions may include troubleshooting, replacement parts, replacement product, store credit, repair instructions, or refund when replacement is not practical. Support should not promise a specific resolution until the claim is reviewed.

Products purchased from unauthorized resellers may not qualify for warranty support. The customer may be asked for proof that the purchase came from an approved channel.

Safety issues such as overheating batteries, smoke, exposed wires, broken glass, or sharp broken parts must be escalated immediately and the customer should be advised to stop using the product.
