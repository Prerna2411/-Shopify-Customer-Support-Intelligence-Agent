Product Manual 1: Smart Travel Backpack

The Smart Travel Backpack includes padded laptop storage, weather-resistant exterior fabric, hidden security pocket, luggage pass-through strap, and USB charging pass-through. The USB port does not include a battery. Customers must connect their own power bank inside the backpack.

Clean with a damp cloth and mild soap. Do not machine wash, bleach, tumble dry, iron, or dry clean. Let the backpack air dry fully before storing electronics inside.

Do not overload the backpack beyond normal travel use. Heavy overload may strain zippers, seams, shoulder straps, and laptop compartments. Damage from overload or misuse is not covered by warranty.

Warranty covers zipper failure, stitching defects, strap defects, and manufacturing issues for 1 year from delivery. Cosmetic dirt, scuffs, stains, accidental tears, water damage from submersion, and airline handling damage are not covered.

If the USB pass-through does not work, ask the customer to test with another cable and power bank before opening a warranty claim.
